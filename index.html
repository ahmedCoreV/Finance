iReceivables are generated live from the Commission tab: each deal becomes a 20/50/30 milestone schedule anchored on its closed month, and milestones flip to Paid as the deal's collected % rises (20% collected pays acceptance, 70% pays through PO, 100% clears delivery). Edit a due date or override Paid here and it sticks; payables are fully manual.
